About Us

Welcome to GEEKGAMES, the ultimate destination for classic gaming enthusiasts! Our mission is to preserve and celebrate classic games as a cultural heritage.

GEEKGAMES offers a curated collection of classic games, preserved and optimized for modern platforms. We believe in collaboration and community-driven development, making our source code freely available for contribution. From iconic titles like XandO and Typemaster from the incredible Mavis Beacon to hidden gems waiting to be unearthed, there's something for everyone here. Join us on this journey to keep the spirit of classic gaming alive and thriving. Let's explore, create, and celebrate the timeless magic of arcade and block games.

Thank you for being a part of the GEEKGAMES an extension of the GEEKCTRL community!
